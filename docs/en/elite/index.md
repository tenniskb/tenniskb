# Elite Level

The deepest level. Reserved for the questions that only surface after years of practice.

## What This

The Elite Level is for the player who has been doing the work for a long time. The strokes are functional. The biomechanics are understood. The patterns are automatic. What remains is the *last* 5% — the questions that don't show up until you've played thousands of matches and trained thousands of hours.

This section is being built slowly, because the questions that live here are the slow ones.

## A Note on the Word "Elite"

I use the word carefully. It doesn't mean "better than everyone." It means "the level at which you have your own questions, not borrowed ones."

If you're still working on grip consistency or footwork, you don't need this section yet — and that's not a criticism. The Foundation and Advanced work is the prerequisite. Come back when the basics are bored, and there will be new questions waiting here for you.
