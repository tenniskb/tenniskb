# Backhand — The Mirror, The Whip & The Slice

The backhand is not a different stroke from your forehand — it's a mirror image. Same unit turn, same chest rotation, same contact point out in front. The direction is reversed, but the mechanics are identical. This guide covers all three backhand variations: the two-handed backhand (the 3.5 default), the one-handed backhand (Dimitrov, Wawrinka, Federer models), and the slice backhand (the 50+ workhorse).
