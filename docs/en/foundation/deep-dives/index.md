## How to Use This Section

These articles are reference material. When a specific question comes up — *why does my backhand break down on high balls? How do I time the serve? What's the right doubles formation here?* — find the article, read it, take it to the court.

The articles are also sequenced. The Complete Manual is the master. The 1-Page Pocket Card is the summary. The individual stroke articles are the deep-dives. Read in that order if you have the time; pick what you need if you don't.
