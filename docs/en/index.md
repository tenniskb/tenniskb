# Tennis Knowledge Base — Your Complete Guide to Playing Better

## Introduction

Welcome to the Tennis Knowledge Base — a comprehensive, bilingual library of practical tennis coaching grounded in real biomechanics and years of on-court experience. Whether you're a 3.5 player building fundamentals or a 5.0+ player refining elite mechanics, you'll find honest, detailed writing about how to play tennis and why the techniques actually work.

Thisn't AI-generated content or quick-fix advice. Every article is written from genuine coaching experience, focused on what your body needs to solve — not what a rigid stroke model demands.
